# Code of Conduct

## Our standard

Be respectful, specific, and constructive. Assume good intent while discussing ideas, but
judge changes by evidence and their effect on users. Harassment, personal attacks,
discrimination, sexualized content, deliberate intimidation, and publishing another person's
private information are not acceptable.

Technical disagreement is welcome. Explain tradeoffs, provide a reproduction where possible,
and criticize the work rather than the person. Maintainers may edit or remove comments and
temporarily or permanently restrict participation when behavior harms the community.

## Scope

This standard applies in repository issues, pull requests, discussions, reviews, and other
spaces where someone is representing the project.

## Reporting

For ordinary moderation, contact the maintainer privately through the contact method on the
[maintainer's GitHub profile](https://github.com/HakimIno). Do not open a public issue that
contains private information. Security vulnerabilities follow the separate private process in
[SECURITY.md](SECURITY.md).

Reports will be reviewed in confidence. Enforcement will be proportional to the behavior,
its impact, and whether it continues after a clear request to stop.
